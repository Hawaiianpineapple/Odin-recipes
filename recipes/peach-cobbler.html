Freash Southern Peach Cobbler

Description

Nice tasty dessert to top off an amazing meal, the peach cobber is a classic after dinner choice.

Ingredients

  • 8 fresh peaches, peeled and pitted
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1 1/4 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/8 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 2 tsp cornstarch
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 6 Tbs unsalted butter, chilled
  • 1/4 boiling water

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F
  2. Combine peaches, 1/4 cup white and brown sugar, 1/4 tsp cinnamon, nutmeg, lemon juice, and cornstarch in large bowl
  3. Add mix to 2-quart baking dish and bake for 10 minutes
  4. Combine flower, 1/4 cup white and brown sugar, backing powder and salt in bowl
  5. Blend in butter until coarse and stir water in until combined mix
  6. Remove peaches from oven and drop spoonfuls of topping over them
  7. Mix 3 Tbs white sugar and 1 tsp cinnamon and sprinkle over entire cobbler
  8. Bake in oven until golden
  9. Plate and serve

Weeknight Wellington

Description

classic Beef Wellington with a twist using mushroom-based spice

Ingredients

  • 5oz Button Mushrooms, coarsely chopped
  • 12oz Portobello Mushrooms, coarsely chopped
  • 1 lb 90% lean ground beef
  • 2 Tbs panko bread crumbs
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp dried thyme, divided
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/8 tsp ground black pepper
  • 4 Tbs butter
  • 2 Shallots
  • 2 Tbs Sherry

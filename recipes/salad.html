Thai Beef Salad

Description

Nice and Easy start to any meal, full of healthy greens and great protein

Ingredients

  • 1 cup lime juice
  • 1 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 cup chopped frest mint leaves
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/3 cup fish sauce
  • 2 green onions chopped
  • 1 stalk lemongrass samashed and cut
    • <1i>1 Tbs sweet chili sauce
  • 1.5 lb 1 inch steak fillet
  • 1 head leaf lettuce
  • 1/2 english cucumber diced
  • 1 pt cherry tomatoes halved

Steps

  1. Mix lime juice,cilantro, mint leaves,sugar,fish sauce, green onions, lemongrass and chili sauce
  2. Preheat grill high heat lightly oil grate
  3. Cook steak medium rare 4-6 min per side with internal heat of 140 degrees
  4. Chop steak, transfer to green mix, cover and refrigerate for 3hrs
  5. Plate and serve
